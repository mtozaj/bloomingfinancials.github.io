About Blooming Financial

Founded in 2024, Blooming Financial is dedicated to providing personalized financial services to small businesses throughout the Bay Area.

Our mission is to help entrepreneurs and small business owners navigate the complexities of financial management, so they can focus on what they do best - growing their business.

We take pride in our local roots and commitment to serving the communities of Cupertino, San Francisco, and surrounding areas with integrity and professionalism.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services

We’re based in the Bay Area and work closely with businesses in Cupertino, San Jose, Mountain View, Sunnyvale, Palo Alto, San Francisco, Berkeley and neighboring communities. In addition, we offer fully remote bookkeeping, payroll, and consulting services to clients anywhere in California, so location is never a barrier to working with us.

Our fees vary depending on the scope and complexity of your needs— things like monthly transaction volume, number of employees, specialized reporting, and any additional consulting or cleanup work. During your free consultation, we’ll review your requirements and provide a transparent, custom quote that fits your budget.

We work daily in QuickBooks Online, Xero, FreshBooks, and Wave, and we’re comfortable adapting to most other cloud accounting tools. If you’re already on a platform you love, we’ll integrate with it—no need to migrate unless you want to.

Catch-up and clean-up projects are a core part of our service. We’ll import your historical bank and credit-card data, recategorize transactions, reconcile every account, and correct any posting or sales-tax errors. Once the books are up to date, we’ll generate year-end financial statements and hand you tidy, audit-ready records—perfect for tax filing or lender requests. Whether you’re a few months behind or several years, we can get you current and keep you current going forward.

Standard clients receive monthly financial reports including profit & loss statements, balance sheets, and cash flow reports. We can customize reporting frequency based your needs. All reports are delivered electronically with clear explanations to help you understand your financial position.

Access is limited to authorized team members who receive regular data-privacy training. We’re happy to sign NDAs, and you retain full ownership of your data at all times.
